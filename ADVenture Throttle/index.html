Adventure Bikes

Himalayan 450

The Royal Enfield Himalayan 450 is a purpose-built, lightweight adventure motorcycle. Priced starting around ₹3.05 Lakhs (ex-showroom), it features RE's first liquid-cooled engine (the 'Sherpa 450'), producing 40 bhp and 40 Nm of torque. It is widely celebrated for its comfortable ergonomics, off-road capability, and modern TFT display.

KTM 390 Adventure X

The KTM 390 Adventure X is a highly capable and value-focused adventure tourer. Featuring the lively 399cc LC4c engine, it punches out 46 hp and 39 Nm of torque. Designed to be a more road-biased and accessible ADV, it features a 19-inch front and 17-inch rear alloy wheel setup.

Honda NX200

The Honda NX200 is an entry-level, adventure-touring motorcycle powered by an OBD-2B compliant 184.4cc, air-cooled engine that produces 17 HP and 15.9 Nm of torque. Priced around ₹1.56 - 1.58 Lakh (ex-showroom), it features a 5-speed gearbox, slipper clutch, dual-channel ABS, and a Bluetooth-enabled TFT display.

TVS Aoache RTX 300

The TVS Apache RTX 300 is an adventure-tourer featuring a 299.1cc liquid-cooled engine, producing 36 PS and 28.5 Nm of torque. Priced between ₹1.99 Lakh and ₹2.29 Lakh (ex-showroom), it comes equipped with a TFT console, traction control, cruise control, and long-travel suspension. It is a versatile choice for city commuting and highway touring.

Hero Xpulse 210

The Hero Xpulse 210 is a highly capable and affordable adventure-touring motorcycle, priced between ₹1.65 Lakh and ₹1.74 Lakh (ex-showroom, Delhi). It is powered by the same 210cc liquid-cooled engine as the Karizma XMR, delivering \(24.6 \text{ PS}\) of power and \(20.7 \text{ Nm}\) of torque. It features an assist-and-slipper clutch, long-travel suspension, and dual-channel ABS.

Yezdi Adventure

The Yezdi Adventure is a rugged, purpose-built adventure tourer featuring a 334cc liquid-cooled engine. Priced starting at ₹1.98 lakh (ex-showroom), it offers excellent low-end torque for off-roading. It is ideal for riders seeking an approachable, feature-rich motorcycle for exploring the outdoors.
